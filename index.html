About

I'm a security engineer and researcher with over 15 years of experience at the intersection of threat intelligence, platform engineering, and automation. My career has traced a path from SOC analyst to staff-level engineer, always oriented toward building the tools and infrastructure that make security teams faster and more effective.

At Google, I lead the architecture and development of internal security tooling platforms for the Threat Intelligence Group. Before that, I spent years at Mandiant and EMC building threat analysis systems, incident response capabilities, and the cloud infrastructure to support them at enterprise scale.

Outside of work, I run a multi-node Kubernetes homelab, maintain a LoRa mesh network across southern New Hampshire, and build agentic AI systems for fun. I believe the best security engineers are the ones who build things.
